# How I deal with AI

I am not an AI guru, I don't think that ai will get much better and I personally refrain from using it as a crutch. I use chatgpt to find documentation or to spot bugs or code smell in my code.

AI is cheap and easy information, the internet does not need more of that.

## What if

If AI continues to get better, then I will have no trouble becoming an AI programmer. The point of ai coding is that it is easy to adopt, which is why I see no need in being an AI "specialist".

## Inclusion

If you are an AI enthusiast, by all means keep doing what you're doing, I just ask that you don't inject AI code into my repositories.
